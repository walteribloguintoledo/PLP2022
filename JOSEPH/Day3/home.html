Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori is an unnaturally fit high school student living in Sendai. On his deathbed, his grandfather instills two powerful messages within Yuji: "always help others" and "die surrounded by people." Yuji's friends at the Occult Club unsealed the talisman, a rotten finger, which attracted Curses to the school.
